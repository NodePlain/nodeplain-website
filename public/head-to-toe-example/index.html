Yes! Head to Toe Therapies is a fully registered NDIS provider. We accept all plan management types including self-managed, plan-managed and NDIA-managed funding. We can also help with reports and reviews.

We specialise in paediatric physiotherapy for children from infants through to young adults (0-21 years). Our team has extensive experience working with children at every developmental stage.

Trexo is a robotic exoskeleton designed for children that enables assisted walking therapy. It helps children with conditions like cerebral palsy practise walking patterns with precise, guided support. We are the only clinic in the ACT offering this technology.

No referral is needed to see a physiotherapist in Australia. However, if you are accessing services through NDIS, Medicare or private health extras, a referral from your GP or paediatrician may help maximise your funding.

Our clinic is located in Tuggeranong, Canberra. We have ample parking and are easily accessible by public transport. Contact us for detailed directions.

Standard sessions are 45-60 minutes. Intensive programs like Therasuit or CME blocks typically run 2-3 hours per day over a multi-week period. We'll recommend the best format during your initial assessment.
